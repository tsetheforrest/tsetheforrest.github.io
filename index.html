Our Story

Andy and Cheryl crossed paths twice before deciding to embark on the same journey.
Cheryl, then in her senior year at Swarthmore College, disregarded the cautionary tales of dating freshmen,
and consequently, Andy and Cheryl grew to become very close companions for some years after.
Having grown (just a bit) older and wiser, they met again in Brooklyn,
and were reminded of why they got along so well the first time.
Since then, they have adopted a jindo pup, become domestic partners,
lived in Brooklyn, Queens and Manhattan, traveled far and wide,
and explored culinary methods with their many kitchen gadgets.
